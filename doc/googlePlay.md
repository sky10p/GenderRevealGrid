```
🍼 The most suspenseful baby gender reveal moment — now in the palm of your hand.

Gender Reveal Grid turns any smartphone into a magical reveal experience. Hide the secret, hand the phone to your guests, and let them tap a 3×3 grid of mystery cards one by one. Every reveal keeps the suspense perfectly balanced… until the very last circle decides everything.

─── HOW IT WORKS ───

1. You — the host — secretly choose the winning gender (Boy or Girl).
2. Tap "Start reveal" and hand the screen to your guests.
3. Guests tap the 9 circular cards one by one. Each flip reveals a Boy or Girl card with a beautiful animation.
4. After 8 reveals the score is always exactly 4–4. Nobody knows what's coming.
5. The 9th card seals the fate — and the celebration overlay erupts with confetti!

─── FEATURES ───

✨ Spoiler-safe game engine
   The reveal sequence is computed so no one can guess the winner early. The balance stays 4–4 right until the final tap.

🎴 Beautiful card flip animations
   Smooth 3D flip animations reveal each card face — light blue for Boy, soft pink for Girl.

🎉 Full-screen celebration overlay
   When the last card is tapped, a stunning confetti explosion fills the screen along with your personalised celebration message.

✏️ Fully customisable messages
   Set your own board intro title and subtitle, and write a personal celebration message that appears on the reveal overlay.

🌈 Visual themes (more coming!)
   Choose the look and feel that matches your party:
   • Cotton Sky — soft pastel clouds and balloons
   • Golden Dust — warm sparkles and dreamy gold tones

📱 Works completely offline
   No internet, no accounts, no data collection. Everything stays on your device.

─── PERFECT FOR ───

• Baby shower parties
• Gender reveal events
• Intimate family moments
• Social media reveal videos

─── PRIVACY ───

Gender Reveal Grid collects no personal data and requires no permissions. Everything runs locally on your device.

─── LANGUAGES ───

Available in English and Spanish.

Make your baby's gender reveal unforgettable. Download Gender Reveal Grid today!
```
